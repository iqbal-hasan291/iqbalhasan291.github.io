About Me

I’m an enthusiastic and detail-oriented entry-level Data Analyst with a strong academic background in Mathematics from Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University. My passion for data analytics grew from a curiosity about how data can be transformed into meaningful insights that drive decisions and create impact. Over time, I have developed hands-on experience with tools like Excel, Google Sheets, MySQL, Power BI, and Python to uncover trends, solve business problems, and craft compelling, data-driven stories. My technical toolkit now also includes Google BigQuery, advanced SQL, and data warehousing concepts, which help me work efficiently with large-scale datasets.

Lately, I’ve been diving deeper into statistical analysis, A/B testing, hypothesis testing, Metabase, and advanced SQL, exploring how data can tell stories and guide smarter business decisions. Each new skill adds a layer of insight, helping me validate assumptions, design experiments, and uncover patterns that truly matter. My focus is on customer segmentation, cohort analysis, and RFM modeling, using these techniques to understand behavior, improve retention, and deliver actionable recommendations. Combining these approaches with my toolkit — Excel, Power BI, Google BigQuery, and Python — allows me to turn complex datasets into meaningful insights that drive impact and growth.
